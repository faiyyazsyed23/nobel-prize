Educational resources

Our educational resources are made up of offerings for both students and teachers. They include animated games and easy to use Nobel Prize lessons. Many Nobel Prize laureates testify that a certain teacher challenged or encouraged them, and that the experience has been crucial to the choices they made in life. Through our educational efforts we want to assure many more students experience the feeling of a genuine aha-moment.
