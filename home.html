In Memoriam

Leon N. Cooper passes away

Physicist Leon N. Cooper passed away on 23 October 2024. He was awarded the 1972 Nobel Prize in Physics for the BCS-theory.

Biography

Andrew V. Schally dies aged 97

Endocrinologist Andrew V. Schally died on 17 October 2024. He was awarded the 1977 Nobel Prize in Physiology or Medicine for his discoveries regarding peptide hormone production.

Biography

Physicist Tsung-Dao Lee dies

Tsung Dao Lee passed away on 4 August, age 97. He was awarded the 1957 Nobel Prize in Physics for his work on parity laws and discoveries regarding elementary particles.

Biography

J. Robin Warren passes away aged 87

J. Robin Warren passed away on 23 July 2024. He was awarded the 2005 Nobel Prize in Medicine for discovering *Helicobacter pylori* and its role in gastritis and peptic ulcers.
